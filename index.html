Our Curriculum

Our tutoring is focused on two areas. The UCVTS exam or just regular middle school tutoring. The UCVTS exam is focused on two essential parts. Math and Reading. Math problems will include questions from 6th-9th grade. Basic algebra, geometry, and fundamentals of math are included. Reading includes reading comprehension and grammar.

First

Student will take practice test that will evaulate the student's strengths and weaknesses. Student will take Khan Academy tests and SSAT tests.

Second

Tutor will then tutor students on their weaknesses and then give them problems to get better at solving them.

Third

Student will then retake these tests after all weaknesses are covered. If these weaknesses appear again on these tests, student will work on them more.
